Welcome To Rock Climbing

Sri Lanka’s impressive mountains, amazing rocks and stunning views make the country a true paradise for climbers. Rock climbing is an exhilarating, yet extreme sport that tests the climber's physical and mental strength, endurance, agility and balance. Join us on adventure tours Sri Lanka as we take you to the heights of excitement and make your climbing experience a memorable one, regardless of whether you’re an amateur or professional.

Being a tropical paradise island, Sri Lanka is filled with and has many natural rock formations. It doesn’t matter if you are a beginner, an experienced professional climber or something in the middle; we have something for everyone. If you are trying out rock climbing for the first time, Sri Lanka is an ideal place to start, as there are various established climbing routes around the country. The country has many simple and safe climbing routes with guides who are ready to help you on your first climb. At the starting point, our professional team will provide you with the safety instructions and equipment - harness and helmet that will ensure a safe climb. Once hooked on to the rope, it’s time to get the adrenaline pumping and start the climb!

There are many varied options and rock types to try out. Most climbing locations would be in the Knuckles Mountain Range, but Sri Lanka does have many rock formations around the central highlands in places like Sigiriya, Dambulla, Adam’s Peak, Nuwara Eliya and the Bambarakanda Falls. The Surathali Ella Waterfall is a short drive from Eco Team’s Adventure Centre in Belihuloya. This area has the most beautiful mountain sceneries with tall trees and lush vegetation. The origin of the river Kadawath Oya lies in the Horton Plains National Park.
